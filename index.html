About USCM Library System

-Book Borrowing and Returning System: Allows students and staff to borrow and return books efficiently.
-Membership Management: Manages library memberships, including student and faculty accounts.
-Advanced Search: Allows users to search by title, author, genre, or publication year.
-Multi-Language Support: Provides library services in multiple languages to accommodate diverse users.
-Seating and Study Room: Facilitates the study spaces or conference rooms.



-Eligibility: Library cards are available to all enrolled students, faculty, and staff members of the university.
-Issuance Process:Cards can be obtained by submitting an online or in-person application at the library front desk.
-Card Validity: Valid throughout the duration of the university course or employment period.
-Access to Resources: Enables borrowing of books,and use of study spaces.
-Lost Card Replacement: Lost or damaged cards can be replaced with a nominal fee.
-Limitations: Borrowing limits and book reservation options depend on user type (e.g., students vs. faculty).
-Security Features:Every card has a unique Ref.No.
